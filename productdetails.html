DESCRIPTION
Simple Refreshing Face Wash is a 100% soap-free, hypoallergenic facial cleanser designed especially for sensitive skin. Its gentle gel-based formula effectively removes makeup, dirt, and other impurities without irritating or drying out the skin. The product is enriched with skin-loving ingredients such as Pro-Vitamin B5, Vitamin E, allantoin, and triple purified water to help replenish and nourish the skin, leaving it soft, smooth, and fresh. Dermatologically and ophthalmologically tested, this face wash is hypoallergenic and non-comedogenic, and contains no artificial perfume, colors, parabens, alcohol, or harsh chemicals. The vegan and cruelty-free formulation makes it suitable for all skin types, especially sensitive, dry, or acne-prone skin.
HOW TO USE
  • Wet your face with warm water.
  • Squeeze out a dime-sized amount of the face wash onto your fingertips.
  • Work it into a gentle lather and massage onto wet skin in circular motions, focusing on areas prone to oiliness or dryness.
  • Avoid direct contact with eyes.
  • Rinse thoroughly with warm water and pat your face dry.
  • Use twice daily, morning and evening, for best results.
